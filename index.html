About

Grafiya is an annual national technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology, SMKFIT. It attracts several thousand students from hundreds of colleges and universities across India. The essence of GRAFIYA is to transcend classrooms and facilitate learning through innovative ideas and mind boggling events. It exposes the hidden talent in the students and also serves as an ideal platform to keep "abreast" of the latest technology and innovations
